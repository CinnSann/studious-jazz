Program Overview

The Msimbo coding program is a (20) week intensive course that meets Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM. Students engage in individual and group activities throughout the sessions to learn the intricacies of software development and coding. The cirriculum covers some of the following topics:

    HTML language
    CCS Styling
    JavaScript

During this time, students will navigate through Script styles and learning Java-code. At the end of 20 weeks, students will have the ability to build their own websites and be proficient web designers in the industry.
